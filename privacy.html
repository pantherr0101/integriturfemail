Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how IntegriTurf collects, uses, and protects your personal information through our website and SMS communications.

Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number when submitted through forms
  • Information related to sample orders or service inquiries
  • Any additional details voluntarily shared by customers during communication

Use of Information

  • To fulfill sample requests and service orders
  • To send SMS notifications if you opt in
  • To respond to inquiries or provide customer service
  • To improve our services and communication

SMS Consent

By submitting your phone number and opting in, you agree to receive text messages from IntegriTurf regarding your order, delivery status, and other important updates. Message frequency may vary. Standard message and data rates may apply. Reply STOP at any time to cancel.

How We Protect Your Information

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. Your data is stored in secure platforms such as Airtable and will never be sold or shared without your consent.

Your Rights

You have the right to request a copy of your personal data, update any inaccuracies, or ask us to delete your information. Contact us anytime at info@integriturf.com.
